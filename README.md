# Project Nebula: Official Documentation

Welcome to **Project Nebula**, a social deduction game inspired by Gnosia. This document covers everything you need to know about playing the game, understanding your role, and mastering the ship's systems.

---

## 1. Objective of the Game

The crew is trapped on a ship with one or more **Gnosia**—aliens that have taken human form.

-   **Humans**: Your goal is to identify all Gnosia and put them into **Cold Sleep** (elimination) before they outnumber the crew.
-   **Gnosia**: Your goal is to eliminate enough crew members so that the Gnosia count is equal to or greater than the human count.

---

## 2. The Game Loop

Each game follows a cycle of Day and Night phases:

### Phase 1: Day Discussion
The crew gathers in the main cabin to talk. This is the time to share information (or lies) and observe everyone's behavior. Use the **Chat Panel** to communicate.

### Phase 2: Voting
After discussion, a voting round begins.
1.  **Nominations**: Players nominate others for Cold Sleep.
2.  **The Vote**: Everyone votes on the nominees. The player with the strict majority of votes is sent to Cold Sleep.
3.  **Ties**: If there is a tie, no one is eliminated for that day.

### Phase 3: Afternoon
A brief transition period where players can have final chats or prepare for the night.

### Phase 4: Night
The most dangerous time.
-   **Gnosia** choose a target to kill.
-   **Special Roles** (Engineer, Doctor, Guardian, etc.) perform their unique night actions.
-   **Humans** wait for dawn, hoping they aren't the target.

### Phase 5: Morning (Results)
The ship wakes up. If someone was killed by Gnosia, it is announced. If a Guardian Angel successfully protected a target, the kill is blocked.

## 3. Roles and Abilities

### Crew-Aligned Roles

| Icon | Role | Description | Ability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| ⚡ | **Engineer** | The ship's scanner specialist. | **Scan**: Check one player at night to see if they are Gnosia. |
| ☤ | **Doctor** | Expert in biological remains. | **Inspect**: Check a player in Cold Sleep to see if they *were* Gnosia. |
| 🛡 | **Guardian Angel** | The silent protector. | **Protect**: Choose one player to guard at night. If Gnosia target them, the kill fails. |
| ⚖ | **Lawyer** | Master of ship protocol. | **Dismiss**: Once per game, you can cancel a Voting round entirely, preventing any elimination. |
| ◈ | **Human** | Standard crew member. | No special ability, but your vote is your greatest weapon. |

### Gnosia-Aligned Roles

| Icon | Role | Description | Ability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 👁 | **Gnosia** | The alien infiltrators. | **Kill**: Vote with other Gnosia to eliminate one player each night. |
| ◈ | **Traitor** | A human who has joined the Gnosia. | **Stealth**: You appear "Human" to Engineer scans and Doctor inspections. You win with Gnosia. |
| ♾ | **Illusionist** | A master of deception. | **Infect**: At the start of the mission, you choose one human to turn into a Gnosia. |
| (Any) | **Shapeshift** | (Illusionist Only) | **Impersonate**: Change your name and appearance in chat to frame others. |

## 4. Special Systems

### Emotes and Auras
-   **Emote Wheel**: Hold your profile image in the lobby or game to open the emote wheel. Selecting an emote plays a short animation for everyone to see.
-   **Auras**: When you win a game or meet certain conditions, you gain **Aura Rolls**. Equip stunning visual effects (like Golden Saiyan, Glitch, or Judgement) to stand out.

### Shared Music
-   The **Host** has control over the ship's music. They can toggle the Lobby and End Game music themes for all players.
-   Individual players can adjust their own volume or mute the music in the side panel.

### Mission Settings (Host Only)
-   **Gnosia Count**: Set the number of Gnosia (manual or auto-calculate based on player count).
-   **Role Toggles**: Customize the match by enabling or disabling characters like the Lawyer or Illusionist.

---

## 5. Strategy Tips

-   **As Engineer**: Don't reveal your scans too early, or the Gnosia will target you. But don't wait too long, or you'll take your secrets to the grave.
-   **As Gnosia**: Use the private Gnosia chat to coordinate kills. Try to blend in with the crew by voting for "obvious" targets.
-   **As Illusionist**: Your infection is powerful because it grows your team. Coordinate with your new ally immediately!
-   **As Lawyer**: Save your dismiss ability for when the crew is about to make a huge mistake (like voting out the real Engineer).
